General Information

A hackathon is an event where participants collaborate on coding projects, typically over the course of several hours or days. Teams work together to develop software or hardware solutions, often centered around a specific theme or challenge.

Attending Lockdown is completely free! Meals and accommodations are provided, though transportation expenses are not covered.

Lockdown is open exclusively to high school students. To apply, simply fill out the Google Form linked at the top.

Team & Participation

You can participate individually or in a team of up to four members. If you don’t have a team beforehand, don’t worry—teams can be formed at the event!

At the start of the hackathon, there will be a team formation session for participants who are looking for teammates.
